Delete your Inner Thread account

Inner Thread · Piedmont Developers

You can delete your Inner Thread account and all associated data at any time, directly from inside the app. The steps below apply to both iOS and Android.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Inner Thread on your phone.
  2. Tap Settings in the bottom tab bar.
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm when prompted. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

What gets deleted

When you tap Delete account, Inner Thread permanently removes:

  • Your account record (email address, account ID)
  • All journal entries you have written or recorded
  • All voice recordings and their transcripts
  • All AI Letters and Ask responses generated from your entries
  • Mood and energy ratings attached to entries

Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days of deletion. After that, no copy of your content remains on our servers.

What we keep, briefly

For audit, fraud-prevention, and tax compliance, we retain a minimal anonymized record of your subscription transactions (purchase date, amount, country) for the period required by law — typically 7 years. This record contains no personally identifying information and cannot be linked back to you after account deletion.

Can't access the app?

If you have lost access to your account or device and cannot reach the in-app delete button, email support@innerthread.net with the subject line "Delete my account" and the email address you used to sign up. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 5 business days.

Heads up: Deletion is permanent. We cannot recover entries, voice recordings, or Letters after the delete-account button is tapped. If you want a copy of your writing first, use Settings → Your data → Export all my data before deleting.
