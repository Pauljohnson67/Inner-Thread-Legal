DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Inner Thread is a private memory journal that helps you think with your past.

Each evening, write or speak the day's entry. Inner Thread reads only your own writing — and uses what you've written before to gently respond, in your own voice. It's like a letter from yesterday's version of you.

— A nightly ritual, not a productivity app
— Voice or text entries, encrypted at rest
— Browse past entries like flipping through a notebook
— Ask anything ("When was the last time I felt rested?") — Inner Thread retrieves the relevant entries and lets the version of you who wrote them speak again
— No streaks. No followers. No ads. Just a page and the time to set things down before sleep.

PRIVACY-FIRST
Your entries are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. We never use your writing to train AI models. Read our full Privacy Policy at https://innerthread.net/privacy.
