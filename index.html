Inner Thread

A private memory journal that helps you think with your past.

A nightly ritual

Each evening, write or speak the day's entry. Inner Thread reads only your own writing — and uses what you've written before to gently respond, in your own voice. It's like a letter from yesterday's version of you.

  • Voice or text entries, encrypted at rest
  • Browse past entries like flipping through a notebook
  • Ask anything — Inner Thread retrieves the entries that matter
  • No streaks. No followers. No ads on premium. Just a page and the time to set things down before sleep.
